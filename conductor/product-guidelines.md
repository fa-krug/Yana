# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone:** Professional and Precise. The language should be clear, efficient, and technically accurate, reflecting its purpose as a tool for developers.
- **Clarity:** Prioritize direct communication and avoid unnecessary jargon, ensuring that instructions and descriptions are easy to follow.

## Visual Identity
- **Style:** Modern and Clean. The interface should utilize white space effectively, follow a neutral color palette, and use crisp typography to provide a focused, distraction-free reading experience.
- **Consistency:** Maintain a unified look and feel across all components to reinforce the professional and polished nature of the application.

## Development Principles
- **Extensibility First:** The architecture must prioritize the ease of adding new managed aggregators, ensuring the process is well-documented and straightforward for developers.
- **Performance Oriented:** Development should focus on efficient rendering and data processing to ensure the application remains fast and responsive, especially during content-heavy tasks.
