# Initial Concept
A modern, fullstack TypeScript/Node.js RSS aggregator with Angular SSR, featuring AI-powered content processing, multiple aggregator types, and a unified codebase.

# Product Guide

## Target Users
Yana is primarily designed for developers who are looking for a modern, self-hosted RSS aggregator that they can control and customize.

## Goals
The core objective of Yana is to simplify RSS management by providing a modern, unified interface that streamlines how users interact with their feeds.

## Core Features
- **Managed Aggregators:** A specialized system where aggregators are created for specific feeds to handle their unique quirks. The architecture allows developers to easily add new managed aggregators.
- **AI-Powered Content Processing:** Integration of AI to summarize and process feed content, helping users quickly digest information.
